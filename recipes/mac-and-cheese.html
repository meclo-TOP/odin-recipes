Mac and Cheese

Description

Golden breadcrumbs crown a sea of creamy, cheesy pasta. Baked until bubbly and steeped in sharp cheddar, this mac delivers the perfect crunch-to-cream payoff in every bite. Ready in 40 minutes, this is comfort food that always steals the spotlight.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups shells pasta
  • 4 Tbsp salted butter
  • 4 Tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 3 cups whole milk (or heavy cream for a extra rich and creamy sauce)
  • 3 cups chredded cheese(s) - sharp cheddar recommended but any combination of desired shredded cheeses will work the same
  • Seasonings - 1/2 Tsp salt, 1/4 Tsp pepper, 1/4 Tsp garlic powder
  • 1/3 cup seasoned panko breadcrumbs - labeled as 'seasoned' or 'italian-style', if using plain panko mix in dried parsley, oregano, and parmesan

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F
  2. Make the roux
    • Melt butter in a medium saucepan
    • Whisk in flour until smoooth
    • Continue cooking for about 2 minutes , stirring constantly
    • Finished once mixture darkens and begins to emit a nutty scent
  3. Make the cheese-sauce / Boil the pasta
    • Into your roux, whisk in milk, salt, pepper, and garlic powder
    • Cook over medium heat until thickened (enough to coat the back of a spoon, 5-7 minutes), while sauce is cooking, boil pasta 2 minutes less than al dente, drain fully
    • Once sauce is thickened, turn off the heat and whisk in 2 cups of shredded cheese
  4. Combine and bake
    • Mix noodles with cheese-sauce
    • Pour into an 11 x 7 baking pan, top with remaining cheese and breadcrumbs
    • Bake for 20 minutes, breadcrumbs should be golden-brown
